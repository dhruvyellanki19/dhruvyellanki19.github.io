## 🎓 Pro Tips

### Before Sharing
1. Send to 2-3 trusted friends for feedback
2. Check on your actual phone, not just emulator
3. Test in incognito/private browsing
4. Clear cache and reload
5. Check from different networks

### Content Tips
1. Use action verbs (Built, Architected, Designed)
2. Include numbers (94% accuracy, 100K queries)
3. Show business impact ($2M saved)
4. Be specific about tech stack
5. Demonstrate scale (millions of records)

### Design Tips
1. Less is more - don't clutter
2. Consistent spacing throughout
3. Readable font sizes (16px+ body)
4. High contrast for readability
5. Test with actual users
